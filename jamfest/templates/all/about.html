Our Bio

Jamhuri Events is an innovative production company providing dynamic event solutions, digital marketing strategies and a talent resource bank in East Africa.
Jamhuri leverages the agency’s unique expertise across music, television, digital media and live entertainment, access to talent and key industries, and powerful marketplace intelligence to create and provide a range of strategic and creative solutions for brands.
