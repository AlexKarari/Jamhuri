Jamhuri Events (Initially known as Jamhuri Festival) was started in 2015 as a platform to nurture, guide and showcase the rich and diverse creative and performing arts culture in Nairobi.
In addition to that, Jamhuri Events is an innovative production company providing dynamic event solutions, digital marketing strategies and a talent resource bank in East Africa. Jamhuri leverages the agency’s unique expertise across music, television, digital media and live entertainment, access to talent and key industries, and powerful marketplace intelligence to create and provide a range of strategic and creative solutions for brands.
It is best known for its conference, live music gigs and festivals that celebrate the convergence of the interactive, arts, and music industries, it has since evolved to include a popular weekly online and concert series dubbed Jamhuri Jam Sessions.
The event, an essential destination for global professionals, features sessions, showcases, exhibitions, and a variety of networking opportunities, proving that the most unexpected discoveries happen when diverse topics and people come together.
